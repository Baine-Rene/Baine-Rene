Who Sam Spratt ?

Sam is a New York based artist working out of his studio in the city. Sam’s pursuit is to make human art with human values about our stumbles, realizations, and fight to engage in a shared reality. Through intricacy at all levels, he seeks to create art within the digital space we actually operate in today that preserves the tactility, code, and values of the space we move through physically. Through focusing that technique towards a human story, his goal is to provide a bridge for the natural world as it extends into a digital network.
