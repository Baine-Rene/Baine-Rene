The YoungEye Foundation

July 16, 2024

What is the YoungEye Foundation ?

Since August 2023, I’ve been working with the foundation to enhance their visual communications. My design work has included creating templates and visuals to showcase their achievements, highlight milestones, and support their mission of positive community impact.

What was my role with this non profit organization ?

In my role with the foundation, I focused on creating designs that incorporated their brand colors and developed engaging social media posts. I aimed to ensure that each design was clear, simple, and effectively communicated our message. For events like donation drives and book giveaways, I worked on turning photos into visually appealing displays that highlighted the foundation’s efforts.

My Work for the Foundations 5 Year Anniversary.

To mark the 5-year milestone of the YoungEye Foundation’s work in the community, I was tasked with creating visuals that reflected their journey and achievements. This involved designing graphics that celebrated their accomplishments and highlighted the impact they’ve made over the years.

5 Year Celebration Post

Campaigns

In celebration of the 5 year milestone the foundation planned to plant five hundred trees at the schools they had donated scholastic materials to. A visual was needed to help raise awareness about the event, I opted to create a design that was simple but sent a message to the foundations target audience.

Planting 500 Trees Event
Book Donation Drive

For this campaign, my primary goal was to keep the foundation’s two colors prominent and ensure the logo was integrated smoothly into the design. Using Figma, I explored a range of typefaces to find the perfect ones for the campaign titles. My focus was on selecting fonts that were both simple and easy to read, making sure the titles were clear and accessible to viewers.

Partnering with other organizations

YoungEye Foundation working with partners

For the tree-planting event, the foundation partnered with other organizations and needed a design that showcased both the YoungEye Foundation’s and the partnering organization’s logos. I created a visual that highlighted the collaboration and ensured both logos were prominently featured, reflecting the spirit of teamwork for the event.
